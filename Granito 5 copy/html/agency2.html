LE NOSTRE RADICI ALPINE

We are deeply committed to protecting the environment. We adopt eco-friendly practices at every stage of our business to minimize environmental impact. We use advanced technologies to optimize the use of resources and minimize waste.

A 130 Year Legacy

With over 130 years of experience, Cristallina® was born in the heart of the Alps Swiss, carrying on a tradition of excellence in the processing of natural stones. Our history begins 250 million years ago, with formation of Cristallina marble in the Triassic period. Modern mining began in 1946 with the construction of the first carriage road and the introduction of technologies innovative such as helical wire cutting machines and chain saws. In 2010, the Maurino family reorganized the quarry, further improving the techniques extractive. Today, Cristallina Marmo extracts 14 different qualities of marble.

Timeless Design

Cristallina® expertly combines artisan tradition and technological innovation to create unique design pieces. Our artisan techniques, refined over the course of over a century, are integrated with the latest technologies to guarantee products of superior quality marble. Each piece is characterized by meticulous care details, expressing our passion for marble and the art of design.
