ECAL per Cristalina®

CRISTAVINO

DESCRIZIONE

I porta vino in marmo Cristallina combinano eleganza e funzionalità, ispirandosi alla ricchezza del territorio ticinese. Ogni pezzo esalta il contrasto cromatico tra il vino e il marmo, mettendo in risalto la purezza dei colori naturali. Ideali per ristoranti, cantine vinicole e ambienti privati.

Design by Alix Malamaire

DETTAGLI

Ø 60 X H 80 Marble Cristallina white venato SWISS MADE

SPEDIZIONI E RESI

CRISTAVINO

Alix Malamaire

My idea for this project is to combine two riches present in the Ticino region, viticulture and marble. This fusion will be done through a wine display, seeking elegance and functionality. This object could be presented on restaurant tables, during experiments in wineries, or at private individuals. The chromatic conctrast between wine and marble in order to bring out the purity of the colors of the marble of Cristallina, playing between sepage and shades of marble. to realise the product, i got inspiratiom from the waterjet marble cutting and from the coring.
