CORALS - COAT HANGER

DESCRIZIONE

L'appendiabiti in marmo Cristallina combina stile e praticità, ispirandosi alle origini marine di questo prezioso marmo bianco. Con un design elegante, è ideale per organizzare i tuoi indumenti, trasformando la bellezza naturale e la storia del marmo Cristallina in un elemento distintivo e sofisticato per la tua casa.

Design by Minjun Choi

SPEDIZIONI E RESI

CORALS

CORALS

Cristallina marble was created in the Triassic period, some 250 million years ago, from the calcification and crystallization of various marine elements, as the sea covered Europe at that time. White marble is in fact the metamorphosis of the remains of corals.” Through this project, I want to convey the origins of Cristallina Marble. It may be a paradoxical expression of Switzerland’s characteristics. This is because Switzerland is surrounded by land and has no sea nearby. However, since the marble originally came from the sea and is now located on top of a mountain, I think this story makes the value of Cristallina marble mysterious and enriched.
